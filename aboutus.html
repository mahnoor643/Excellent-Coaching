ABOUT US






Excellent is one of Pakistan’s best known & the most experienced IT and English language learning centre with great choice of study programs specifically designed according to students’ needs & demands. In Karachi, our Campuses are situated at Gulshan-e-Iqbal, Defence, Bahadurabad , Nazimabad and UP More.

OUR SPECIALITY

We provide a comprehensive suite of English language program through our specially designed series of English language learning books that help students / learners immensely. Backed by years of research, our program supports teachers & decision makers & help individuals achieve academic, business, and personal success with English language proficiency.

WE CARE

Excellent caters the needs of everyone with superb range of English Language courses,IT & Biotech excellent environment & most professional care possible. We offer courses to suit everyone’s need from scratch level to advanced.

The programs offered for english language are Pre-Foundation, Foundation, Elementary, Pre-Intermediate, Intermediate, S.L.E, IELTS, and teacher training workshops. Apart from IELTS & Advance courses, duration of all courses is 3 hours a day in which students learn Grammar, Conversation and Audio-Visual sections for better understanding and improving accent. Excellent is the 1st English language centre in Pakistan which offers English language courses using an audio-visual technology and headphone system. Audio-Visual classes are specially designed to fulfil the needs of audio training for students. The programs offered for IT are Ms Office and Internet , Graphic designing , Web designing , Web development , Video editing , Digital media marketing , Computer typing.
